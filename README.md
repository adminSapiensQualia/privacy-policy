Privacy Policy for Board Games Pool
Effective Date: March 5, 2026

1. Introduction
Sapiens Qualia ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Board Games Pool mobile application (the "App"). We believe that your digital privacy is a fundamental right. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

2. Data Collection and Use (Zero Data Policy)
Our stance on privacy is simple: We do not collect, store, transmit, track, or share any personal data.
Board Games Pool is a fully self-contained, offline experience. The App does not require an active internet connection to function. We do not use any hidden tracking scripts, we do not require you to create an account, and we do not monitor your gameplay behavior.

3. Local Storage and Game Progress
Any data generated by the App—such as your in-game bankroll, Artificial Intelligence difficulty settings, or match history—is saved strictly and securely on your own physical device's internal storage. This data never leaves your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties. If you delete the App, this local save data is permanently deleted with it.

4. Third-Party Services, Analytics, and Advertisements
This App is a premium standalone product. It contains:
* NO third-party analytics tools (such as Google Analytics).
* NO third-party advertising networks or ad-targeting SDKs.
* NO social media tracking pixels.
* NO crash reporting software that collects device identifiers.

5. Children's Privacy
Because our App does not collect any personal information whatsoever, it is completely safe for children of all ages and is fully compliant with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR-K). We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time if we introduce new features. However, our core philosophy will never change: we do not want your data. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
